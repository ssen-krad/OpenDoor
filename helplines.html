📞 Support & Resources

Helplines, Shelters & Support

Verified, active contacts across India. All services listed are free for survivors of domestic violence.

🆘 Crisis & Emergency Helplines

Women's Helpline (National)

24/7 emergency support for women facing violence, threats, or harassment. Can arrange transport, police coordination, and shelter placement.

📞 181 (Free, 24/7)

All Emergencies (ERSS)

Police, fire, and ambulance. Works from any mobile — even without SIM balance or a SIM card.

📞 112

Police

Direct police line. State your address clearly and say "I need police assistance immediately."

📞 100

Childline

For children and teens in danger, or adults concerned about a child's safety. Free, 24/7, confidential.

📞 1098 (Free, 24/7)

Elderline

National helpline for senior citizens facing abuse, neglect, or financial exploitation by family members.

📞 14567 (8am–8pm, 7 days)

Cyber Crime Helpline

For digital harassment, online blackmail, non-consensual image sharing, and cyberstalking.

📞 1930

🏛️ Complaints & Legal Reporting

National Commission for Women (NCW)

File online complaints about domestic violence, harassment, or rights violations. WhatsApp helpline also available.

File Complaint Online

📞 Helpline: 14490 (24/7)

National Cyber Crime Portal

File cybercrime complaints 24/7. Use "Report Anonymous" for sexual image abuse — no name required.

cybercrime.gov.in

Protection Officer (PWDVA)

Every district has a Protection Officer who can file your DV Act complaint and connect you to a Magistrate. Accessible via 181 or your district court.

📞 181 (to connect)

🏠 Shelter Homes (Government)

Swadhar Greh Scheme

Government-run shelters in every district. Free accommodation, food, legal support, and counselling for women in distress. Call 181 to be connected to the nearest one.

📞 181 (For referral)

One Stop Centres (Sakhi)

Government centres attached to hospitals in every district. Provide medical aid, police assistance, legal aid, and temporary shelter — all under one roof. No charge.

📞 181 (For referral)

Short Stay Homes

Run by Ministry of Women & Child Development. Short-term accommodation for women and children leaving violent homes. Contact your district Social Welfare Office.

📞 181 (For referral)

🧠 Mental Health & Counselling

iCall (TISS)

Free psychological counselling for trauma from abuse. Trained in domestic violence. Mon–Sat 10am–8pm. Confidential.

📞 9152987821

Vandrevala Foundation

Free 24/7 mental health helpline. Handles crisis situations, trauma, and emotional distress.

📞 +91-9999-666-555 (24/7)

SNEHI

Free 24/7 emotional support helpline. Provides crisis counselling and peer support.

📞 044-24640050

iCall Email Support

For those who cannot speak freely on the phone. Email-based counselling, confidential.

icall@tiss.edu

⚖️ Free Legal Aid

NALSA — National Legal Services Authority

Every person below a certain income threshold is entitled to free legal aid in India. Contact your District Legal Services Authority (DLSA) for a free lawyer.

nalsa.gov.in

Majlis Legal Centre (Mumbai)

Founded by advocate Flavia Agnes. Legal counselling and representation for domestic violence, matrimonial rights, and sexual abuse. Pay-as-you-can model.

majlislaw.com

IT for Change

Technology and gender rights research and advocacy. Supports survivors of tech-facilitated gender-based violence in India.

itforchange.net

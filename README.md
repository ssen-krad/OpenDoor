# Open Door — Domestic Violence Resource Kit for India

**A free, private, bilingual digital safety resource for victims of domestic violence in India.**

> Built for women, teenagers, elders, and families — in English and Hindi.  
> Tracker-free. Cookie-free. Zero-knowledge. GitHub Pages ready.

---

## What This Is

Open Door is a **static information website** modeled after the [SafeStep](https://ssen-krad.github.io/SafeStep/index.html) digital safety toolkit. It helps survivors of domestic violence in India:

- **Identify** abuse patterns across all relationship types
- **Understand** their legal rights (PWDVA 2005, IPC 498A, Zero FIR, Stridhan)
- **Plan** a safe exit with step-by-step guides
- **Find** verified helplines, shelters, and free legal aid
- **Stay safe digitally** — spyware detection, account security, Aadhaar risks
- **Access support** for teen safety, elder abuse, and financial independence

### Safety Features
- **Quick Exit button** — fixed top-right on every page, instantly navigates to Google
- **Single Escape key** — pressing `Esc` once exits to Google
- **History replacement** — `window.location.replace()` means the back button won't return to this site
- **Google favicon** — browser tab shows the Google logo to anyone glancing at the screen
- **Esc hint in footer** — subtle reminder for users
